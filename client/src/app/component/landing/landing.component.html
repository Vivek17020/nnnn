Our Heritage

Born From the Heart of India

Founded in 2012 with a vision to make air travel accessible to every Indian, Bharat Airlines has grown from a regional carrier into one of the country's most trusted aviation brands. We carry the warmth of Indian hospitality at every altitude.

With a fleet of 85 modern aircraft and a team of over 8,000 dedicated professionals, we serve 48 destinations across India — connecting families, businesses and cultures every single day.

Step into a world designed for your comfort. Our aircraft interiors feature spacious reclinable seats, mood lighting inspired by Indian sunsets, and bespoke in-flight entertainment curated to delight every age group and taste.
